Label Gear Solid (v0.1)

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move. Try to get through your day without being labeled by other people.

If it gets too difficult, you can reset the game by clicking the icon in the top-left corner.

Created by Michael Suen (@poetichentai) and Stephen Suen (@s2tephen) for the 2014 QUILTBAG Jam at MIT.
